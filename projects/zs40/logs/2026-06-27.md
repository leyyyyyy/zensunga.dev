# Idea Formation

**2026-06-27**

The biggest problem of my highschool life was my horridly heavy bag that I carried with me everywhere. This bag sucked my soul dry ESPECIALLY since I had a 2 hour commute everyday. Then I thought to myself; "damn, i should reduce the amount of shit in here."
<br>
<br>
introducing the bright idea of minimalizing everything, starting with my hunky clunky keyboard.
<br>
<br>
i've been wanting to create my own custom keyboard for a while, but I've always put it off in lieu of other responsibilities in my life. today marks the day where i wipe the dust off my thinking cap and create something cool. i predict the main problems i'll run into will be regarding the firmware and picking the parts, but that'll go away with a few youtube tutorials.
<br>
<br>
as of now, these are the parts that I have decided on:

**Switches**: Kailh Choch Keyboard Switches <br>
**Keycaps**: Kailh Low Profile Keycaps<br>
**Diodes**: FR107 1A/1000V<br>
**Microcontroller**: NRF52840<br>
**Case**: 3D Printed PLA<br>
**Battery**: Li Ion 3.7V 500mAh <br>
<br>
the next step will deciding the keyboard layout, then designing the case.
